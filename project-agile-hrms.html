Overview

The Agile HRMS project focused on transforming HR operations through structured Agile methodologies and quality assurance practices. The system covers the complete HR lifecycle from recruitment and onboarding to payroll, training, and employee exit processes.

I led the team through three focused sprints, implementing proper user story structures, structured stand-ups, stakeholder reviews, and QA automation. The project resulted in significant improvements in sprint velocity, defect reduction, and overall stakeholder satisfaction.
